## Interview Notes – Day 4

### Key One-Liners
- "Time complexity measures growth, not exact time."
- "Worst case is usually asked unless specified."
- "Nested loops multiply, sequential loops add."
- "Stack space counts in recursion."

### Common Traps
- n + n/2 + n/4 = O(n)
- log inside loop ≠ log overall
- Space is peak usage, not total allocation
